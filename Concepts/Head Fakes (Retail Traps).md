# Head Fakes (Retail Traps)

## Pattern
Price sweeps above a level → looks like a breakout → lacks follow-through → rolls back inside value.

## Signs of a Head Fake

- Sweep is punchy but immediately dies
- Minimal follow-through after the sweep
- Price starts drifting lower (weakness)
- "Farty response through the extreme"

## Mechanism
Retail gets trapped buying the breakout. The breakout fails to attract real momentum.

## The Setup

1. Level is tested and swept
2. Volume came in above the level (HVN formed)
3. Price trades back below it → back inside previous value
4. First target: POC (midpoint)
5. Second target: range lows

## Why It Works
The market is in balance. Attempts to break are absorbed. Early attempts are more likely to fail than succeed.
