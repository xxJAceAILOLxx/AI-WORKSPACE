# Delta Divergence

## One-Line Summary
When price and delta move in opposite directions — the earliest warning signal of a reversal.

---

## What It Is

Delta = net difference between buying and selling volume at each price.

Delta Divergence = price makes new extreme but delta doesn't confirm.

> "Delta divergence frequently appears at the same levels where reversal patterns form — providing earlier confirmation than price action alone."

---

## Two Types

### Bearish Divergence
- Price makes **new highs**
- Delta shows **declining positive delta** (or turning negative)
- Meaning: buyers losing conviction at higher prices
- Signal: reversal likely

### Bullish Divergence
- Price makes **new lows**
- Delta shows **declining negative delta** (or turning positive)
- Meaning: sellers losing conviction at lower prices
- Signal: reversal likely

---

## How to Read It

| Price Action | Delta Action | Interpretation |
|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| New high | Positive but declining | Buyers weakening — bearish divergence |
| New high | Negative | Strong bearish divergence — reversal imminent |
| New low | Negative but declining | Sellers weakening — bullish divergence |
| New low | Positive | Strong bullish divergence — reversal imminent |

---

## Why It Works

- Shows when the aggressor side is losing power
- Earlier signal than candlestick patterns alone
- Combines with structural levels for high-probability setups

---

## Integration with Volume Profile

| Scenario | Delta Divergence Signal |
|----------|------------------------|
| Price at VAH | Bearish divergence → fade VAH |
| Price at VAL | Bullish divergence → fade VAL |
| Price at POC | Divergence at POC → reversal zone |
| Post-breakout retest | Divergence on retest → throwback confirmation |

---

## Key Nuance

- Divergence alone is not a trade — it's a warning
- Must combine with structural context (where) and absorption (how)
- Strongest when it occurs at key profile levels
