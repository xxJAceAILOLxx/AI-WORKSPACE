# Value Area

## Definition
~68-70% of total volume for a given session.

## Boundaries
- **Value Area High (VAH)** — upper bound
- **Value Area Low (VAL)** — lower bound
- **Point of Control (POC)** — the single price level with the highest volume within the value area

## Statistical Analogy
Classic one standard deviation of the distribution.

## What It Represents
- Where the market found the most agreement
- Where the bulk of two-way trade occurred
