# Markov Property

> The future state depends only on the current state, not the sequence of events that preceded it.

---

## Definition

Where the market goes next is entirely dependent on the state today. Past price history does not influence future outcomes—only the current classification matters.

## Application to Trading

1. **Classify current state** (bull/bear/sideways) using recent price action
2. **Calculate transition probabilities** from historical state changes
3. **Use current state** to predict next state likelihood

## Why It Works

Markets exhibit regime persistence. A trending market tends to continue trending until a clear state change occurs.
