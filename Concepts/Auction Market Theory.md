# Auction Market Theory

## Core Idea
Markets function as an auction — continuous search for price acceptance.

---

## Three Phases

| Phase | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Balancing** | Price finds acceptance within a range. Two-way trade. Value area forms. |
| **Trending** | Price rejects one side, moves directionally. Low volume nodes form. |
| **Distribution** | Market is spreading out, losing agreement. Wider ranges, less overlap. |
