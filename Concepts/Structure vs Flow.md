# Structure vs Flow

## The Framework

> "Profile gives you the areas of interest. But it's the actual **order flow** that tells you whether or not you should care."

| | Structure | Flow |
|---|---|---|
| **What** | Volume profile levels (HVN, LVN, POC, value area) | Real-time price action, momentum, absorption |
| **Role** | Where to look | Whether to act |
| **Gives you** | Areas of interest | Conviction and timing |

## Always Together

Structure without flow = guessing.
Flow without structure = no roadmap.

## Example

A POC may show where traders are positioned. But flow tells you:

- Is buying being absorbed at this level? → Don't buy
- Is selling drying up at this level? → Look for reversal
- Is the market punching through with conviction? → Real break, not head fake
