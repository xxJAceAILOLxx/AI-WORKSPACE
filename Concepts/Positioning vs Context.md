# Positioning vs Context

## The Core Distinction

**Positioning** — Short-term. Relevant live positions that traders will act on when price returns. Applicable within minutes to a few sessions.

**Context** — Long-term. The structure, regime, and character of the market. Informative but not directly executable.

---

## Why It Matters

Confusing context for execution is the #1 reason traders get in trouble using volume profile.

| | Positioning | Context |
|---|---|---|
| **Timeframe** | Intraday to a few sessions | Weeks to months |
| **Reliability** | High — participants are still in position | Low — turnover is likely |
| **Actionability** | Immediate — expect reactions at levels | Strategic — shorelines, not lines in the sand |
| **Tool** | Short-term profiles | Longer-term / monthly profiles |
