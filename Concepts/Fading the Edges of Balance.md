# Fading the Edges of Balance

## Setup
- Market is favoring a defined range
- Price has been cut off at VAH and VAL repeatedly
- Expect mean reversion to continue

## Execution
Buy/sell a return to VAH or VAL — expecting rejection.

## Key Nuance

> "The more times that some level is tested, the less I'm going to trust it."

| Attempt | Reliability |
|---------|------------|
| 1st test | High — information |
| 2nd test | Good |
| 3rd+ test | Declining — buildup of pressure |

## Why It Degrades
> "Early tests are information. Repeated tests, repeated tests along the same level, that's just a buildup of pressure. The longer that we spend sideways within a structure, the more likely that when it resolves outside of that structure, especially if violently, the more likely that will lead to a more significant response."
