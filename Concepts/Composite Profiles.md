# Composite Profiles

## When to Use

- Market is trading tightly within a well-defined range
- Individual profiles have **significant overlap**
- The market is truly compressed

## When NOT to Use

- Market has dislocation and loose trading
- Profiles are spread out across a wide range
- There's value migration (shifting from one level to another)

> "The tighter the better."

## If Market is Loose

- Composites are less useful
- Extremes become more actionable than the value area
- No single HVN or POC carries more weight

## The Underlying Reason

> "This tells us the market has loosened up. The market has not found agreement. The market is not balanced currently."
